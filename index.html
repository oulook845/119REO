119REO

The 119REO product's birth journey begins with the fire department. The collected fire suits are cleaned through washing,
leaving some traces of life-saving memories and cleaning them neatly. The washed fire suits are made into fabrics that can be made by breaking down
reflectors, zippers, and other accessories, from the pockets that contained the lifesaving equipment.
